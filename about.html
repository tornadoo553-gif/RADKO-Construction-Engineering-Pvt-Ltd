About RADKO

Company Story

RADKO Construction is a multidisciplinary Mechanical and Civil Engineering company specializing in Construction, Liaisoning, Planning, Real Estate Management, Logistics, and Manpower Supply. Under the leadership of Mr. Pradeep Sahoo, the company has been delivering reliable and professional engineering solutions across a wide range of industrial, commercial, and civil projects.

With a dedicated team of highly motivated, qualified, and technically skilled engineers, supervisors, and workforce, RADKO is committed to executing every project with professionalism, efficiency, and quality. We believe in building long-term relationships through trust, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Our goal is to provide dependable construction and engineering services that meet the highest standards of quality while delivering projects safely, efficiently, and within the expected timelines. Every assignment is handled with responsibility, technical expertise, and a strong commitment to exceeding our clients' expectations.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in the engineering and construction industry by delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable solutions that create lasting value for our clients, communities, and future generations. We strive to build long-term relationships through quality workmanship, timely project delivery, and continuous improvement in every aspect of our business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality engineering and construction services while maintaining the highest standards of transparency, commitment, trust, integrity, quality, and professionalism. We believe that our people are our greatest strength, our customers are our highest priority, and excellence is achieved through teamwork, technical expertise, and customer-focused solutions. Every project is executed with a commitment to safety, quality, timely completion, and complete client satisfaction.
